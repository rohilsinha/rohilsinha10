The Problem

With an average daily social media usage of 143 minutes, users often encounter distracting or irrelevant content. This leads to missed opportunities for valuable learning and misinformation spreading.

Additionally, new internet users face challenges accessing credible educational resources, further widening education gaps.

Introducing SARIT

SARIT curates high-quality educational content, removing distractions and misinformation. It delivers personalized feeds with videos, articles, podcasts, webinars, and innovative formats.

Key Features

  • ManimBooks: Interactive e-books with animations and dynamic visuals.
  • Learning Circles: AI-powered micro-communities for mentorship and collaboration.
  • Discover Mode: Thematic learning journeys combining multimedia.
  • Mind Map Navigator: Visualize and connect topics for holistic learning.

Future Innovations

  • Gamified learning quests with interactive challenges and rewards.
  • Virtual study groups for real-time collaboration.
  • AR-powered lessons for immersive education.
  • Content Creator Marketplace to empower educators.
